Courtney is a features food and lifestyle writer contributing to glossy magazines in East Africa, and the United States.

Since 2004, she has been a monthly food and lifestyle columnist for the glossy magazine Travel News and Lifestyle (TN East Africa). The magazine is disributed throughout East Africa. Courtney has also written restaurant reviews and has styled food shots for TN and other East African publications.

Courtney is the “Kenya Field Expert” writing vignettes of life in Kenya for the exclusive travel club My Little Swans which hosts a web magazine. Her work specializing in home design has also appeared in Orange Coast magazine. More recently, she has written the copy for the La Villa del Valle website, a boutique country hotel in Baja California and has authored the web pages for Vena Cava Wines and Baja Botanica beauty products, writing in English and Spanish.

Born in México City, she has lived and worked in Madrid, Paris, New York, Los Angeles and Kenya. Courtney currently lives in Corona del Mar, California where she is discovering the exciting and newly emerging flavours of Mexican Cuisine.
